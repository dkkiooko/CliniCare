About Us

As a physician working in the public sector, I have witnessed firsthand the detrimental effects of an outdated paper-based system on patient care. The lack of an electronic system has resulted in delayed clinical care, misplaced medical records, and a sense of frustration among both patients and colleagues.

When thinking of ideas to work on for our portfolio project, we discovered that this issue is present in both our countries, Ethiopia and Kenya.

To address this challenge, we embarked on a mission to develop an Electronic Medical Record (EMR) system that can be implemented in small health facilities, significantly improving the quality of care provided. Our aim is to ensure that patients receive timely and efficient care, while also streamlining medical records for ease of access and retrieval.

We begun development of the first version of CliniCare EMR starting from March 10, 2023. Which followed a rigorous three-week research period from February 17, 2023, to March 9, 2023, where we explored various options and strategies to ensure the success of our project. We are confident that our system will have a hand in providing a reliable, efficient, and user-friendly platform for medical professionals to deliver quality care to their patients.
